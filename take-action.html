Next Steps

There are many ways in which you can improve your global education on a day-to-day basis.

  • Read articles from foreign newspapers
  • Participate in or engage with educational conferences
  • Search for your own implicit biases and seek to understand why they exist
  • Keep questioning what you hear, learn, and believe to be true. Is there another valid perspective?
